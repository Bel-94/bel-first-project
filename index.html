About me

My background

I was born and raised in Nanyuki. My parents were blessed with three children, with me being the first born. Growing up was not easy as we are of a humble background. But our parents did everything they could to give us a comfortable life. At times we could not afford to eat three meals a day; sometimes we would sleep hungry or even wake up to no breakfast at all. Our parents gave us hope for a better tomorrow and they ensured we concentrated in our books, as they say"education is the key."

Education

Getting the best education, is the dream of every parent for the child. My parents are not any different. They struggled to put me in a good school where they believed is where I would get a foundation of a better life in future.I dedicated myself to perform well and grasp everything we were being taught so as not to disappoint my parents.
